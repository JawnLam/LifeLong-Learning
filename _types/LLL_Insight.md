# <What was learned or established>

## The insight

*One to three sentences: what was learned, or what prior knowledge / corrected misconception was established — stated as a durable fact about **this learner**, not about the topic. This is a decision-grade record, not a session log.*

## Why it changes future teaching

*What this unlocks, rules out, or predicts. A `misconception-corrected` insight predicts related future stumbles (preempt them). A `mastery-floor` raises where to start. A disclosed `prior-knowledge` means don't re-teach it. A `mission-shift` re-points the whole cartridge — cross-link the manifest.*

## Evidence

*How it was demonstrated — a question answered, an activity completed, prior experience cited. Optional, but record it when the claim might be revisited or challenged later.*
